Solutions

WE RESPECT CPAS FOR ALL what THEY DO! WE WANT THEM TO DO MUCH MORE, IN EVERY SENSE, BUT ONE!

It’s a profession that ain’t everyone’s cup of tea! From ensuring timely & accurate bookkeeping along with managing payroll, invoices, payments of each client, the ever-changing regulations, a CPA firm ought to be on top of everything, in order to build and sustain their repertoire.

At Bikham Finance, we are always dedicated towards making each and every CPA practice a smooth sail. With the number of clients increasing at a CPA firm, staying on top of administrative and financial tasks gets overwhelming and usually comes at the expense of invaluable consulting time. That’s where we come in. We completely take the administrative and routing financial tasks off your plate, so you can effectively focus on advisory and get more clients on-board.

Ready to take your practice to the next level?
Let us show you how.

On offer is a comprehensive white label solution, customizable as per your practice. Our focus is entirely towards optimizing your practice; enhancing client experience while lowering costs and helping you make more money!

Bikham Finance is a leader in outsourced accounting solutions for CPA firms, boosting the profitability and capacity of a practice by augmenting our white label team, which takes over the routine maintenance of accounts payable and receivables, general bookkeeping, payroll and tax preparation for all your clients.

A few advantages our clients experience:

  1. Improved financials and boosted growth.
  2. Improved reporting capabilities
  3. Timely delivery of monthly/quarterly/yearly books
  4. Effective communication wherever, whenever.
  5. Reduced reconciliation efforts.
