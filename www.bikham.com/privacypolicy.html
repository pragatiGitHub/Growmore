  • 5. Cookies

    • A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user’s computer for record-keeping purposes. We do use cookies on this site and may link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site. We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. Session cookies make it easier for you to navigate our site but will expire when you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time, but you can remove them by following directions provided in your Internet browser’s “help” file. We set a persistent cookie to store your passwords and/or preferences, so you don’t have to enter it more than once. These cookies also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance their experience on our site.

    If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some areas of our site will be limited. Some of our business partners and clients use cookies on our website. Bikham Information Technology has no access or control over these cookies. This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by our network of sites and does not cover the use of cookies by any client or business partner.
